# **Business Introduction**
# **Business Introduction**

RetailNova is a retail business that sells products across multiple consumer goods categories, including Bakery, Beverages, Fresh Produce, Home Cleaning, Personal Care, Snacks, and Stationery. The company serves customers across different locations, including the USA, Mexico, Germany, France, and Canada.

This project analyzes RetailNova’s business performance using three Excel dashboard screenshots: the Revenue & Profit Performance Dashboard, Product Performance Dashboard, and Customer Performance Dashboard. These dashboards summarize key business areas such as revenue, profit, product performance, customer spending, age group performance, and location-based performance.

The purpose of this project is to transform RetailNova’s business data into meaningful insights that can support better decision-making, improve performance monitoring, and identify opportunities for growth.

---

# Problem Statement

RetailNova needs a clear understanding of its revenue, profit, product, and customer performance. Without a structured dashboard analysis, it can be difficult to identify which products generate the most revenue, which customer groups contribute the most, and which areas of the business require improvement.

The business needs to answer the following questions:

- What is the total revenue, profit, and profit margin?
- Which months performed best in terms of sales and profit?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which products are performing well and which are underperforming?
- Which customers spend the most?
- Which age groups and locations contribute most to revenue?
- What actions can improve business growth and profitability?

# Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing RetailNova’s performance using a three-layered dashboard reporting system built in Microsoft Excel and presented as static dashboard screenshots.

The three dashboards used in this project are:

- **Revenue & Profit Performance Dashboard**
- **Product Performance Dashboard**
- **Customer Performance Dashboard**

Each dashboard addresses a core business area and presents key insights through:

- KPI cards
- Charts and visual summaries
- Slicers for Date, Year, and Product Category

- Dashboard screenshots for documentation and portfolio presentation

The analysis covers RetailNova’s revenue, profit, transactions, products, customers, age groups, and locations. The objective is to help RetailNova’s stakeholders monitor business performance, identify trends, and make informed strategic decisions.

# Revenue & Profit Performance Dashboard Analysis



The Revenue & Profit Performance Dashboard is the first dashboard in the project. It provides a high-level view of RetailNova’s overall financial performance by analyzing total revenue, total profit, profit margin, total transactions, monthly sales, monthly profit trends, product category contribution, location performance, and age group performance.

## Key Performance Indicators

The dashboard shows the following major business metrics:

- **Total Revenue:** $1.66B
- **Total Profit:** $541.0M
- **Profit Margin:** 32.5%
- **Total Transactions:** 51.1K

These figures show that RetailNova generated strong revenue and maintained a healthy profit margin. A profit margin of 32.5% indicates that the business is retaining a good portion of its revenue as profit.

## Monthly Sales Performance

The monthly sales comparison shows that sales performance varied across the year. Sales increased strongly toward the final quarter, especially around October, November, and December.

This suggests that RetailNova experienced stronger customer demand toward the end of the year. This may be linked to seasonal shopping behavior, promotional campaigns, or increased customer purchasing activity during the final quarter.

## Profit Trend Analysis

The monthly profit trend follows a similar pattern to revenue performance. Profit increased during the later months of the year, with December showing one of the strongest profit results.

**Insight:** Profitability is closely connected to sales volume. RetailNova should study the strategies used during high-performing months and apply similar actions during slower periods.

## Revenue Performance by Product Category

The dashboard shows revenue contribution across product categories:

- **Personal Care:** 18.17%
- **Home Cleaning:** 12.39%
- **Beverages:** 8.22%
- **Bakery:** 6.99%
- **Fresh Produce:** 6.41%
- **Stationery:** 3.63%
- **Snacks:** 2.18%

Personal Care generated the highest revenue contribution, making it the strongest product category. Home Cleaning also performed well.

Snacks and Stationery had the lowest revenue contribution, which indicates that these categories may need further review and improvement.

## Revenue by Location

Revenue performance is fairly balanced across the five locations:

- **Canada:** 20.40%
- **France:** 19.99%
- **USA:** 19.92%
- **Mexico:** 19.87%
- **Germany:** 19.81%

Canada recorded the highest revenue percentage, while Germany recorded the lowest. However, the difference between the countries is small, showing that RetailNova has a stable geographic revenue distribution.

## Revenue by Age Group

The dashboard also shows revenue by customer age group:

- **Senior:** $360.5M
- **Old Adult:** $346.1M
- **Elder:** $323.2M
- **Young Adult:** $318.1M
- **Adult:** $315.5M

Senior customers generated the highest revenue, followed by Old Adult customers. This shows that older customer groups are important revenue contributors for RetailNova.

# Recommendation

Based on the dashboard analysis, the following recommendations are proposed:

## 1. Strengthen High-Performing Product Categories

RetailNova should continue investing in Personal Care and Home Cleaning because they generate the highest revenue.

Recommended actions:

- Increase stock availability for best-selling products
- Promote top-performing products more frequently
- Expand product variety in high-performing categories
- Use Personal Care and Home Cleaning products in marketing campaigns

## 2. Improve Low-Performing Product Categories

Snacks and Stationery are the lowest-performing categories and should be reviewed.

Recommended actions:

- Review product pricing
- Improve product visibility
- Run promotional discounts
- Bundle weaker products with stronger products
- Replace products that continue to underperform

## 3. Retain High-Value Customers

RetailNova should develop retention strategies for customers with high average spending.

Recommended actions:

- Create a VIP loyalty program
- Offer personalized discounts
- Send product recommendations
- Provide early access to promotions

## 4. Target Senior and Old Adult Customers

Senior and Old Adult customers generate the highest revenue. RetailNova should continue serving these segments with relevant products and offers.

Recommended actions:

- Promote essential personal care and household products
- Offer repeat-purchase rewards
- Create simple and direct promotional messages
- Provide value bundles for frequent purchases

## 5. Increase Revenue from Younger Customers

Young Adult and Adult customers generate lower revenue compared to older customers.

Recommended actions:

- Use social media campaigns
- Offer youth-focused discounts
- Promote convenience-based products
- Create digital campaigns targeted at younger customers

## 6. Prepare for Strong Year-End Sales

Sales, profit, and units sold are stronger toward the end of the year.

Recommended actions:

- Increase inventory before October, November, and December
- Launch holiday promotions
- Offer seasonal bundles
- Increase marketing during Q4
- Repeat successful December strategies in slower months

# Conclusion

The RetailNova dashboard project provides a clear and structured analysis of revenue, profit, product, and customer performance.

The Revenue & Profit Dashboard shows that RetailNova generated **$1.66B in total revenue**, **$541.0M in total profit**, and achieved a **32.5% profit margin**. The company also recorded **51.1K transactions**, with stronger performance toward the end of the year.

The Product Dashboard shows that **Personal Care** and **Home Cleaning** are the strongest product categories, while **Snacks** and **Stationery** are the weakest categories. Best-performing products include Shea Body Lotion 400ml, Mint Toothpaste 100ml, Aloe Hand Wash 500ml, and Men’s Deodorant 150ml.

The Customer Dashboard shows that high-value customers and older customer groups are important to RetailNova’s performance. Senior and Old Adult customers generated the highest revenue, while customer volume remained balanced across different locations.

Overall, RetailNova is performing well, but there are clear opportunities to improve weaker product categories, retain high-value customers, grow younger customer segments, and maximize year-end sales opportunities.

By applying these insights, RetailNova can improve revenue growth, strengthen profitability, and make better data-driven business decisions.
